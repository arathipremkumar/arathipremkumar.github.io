Avocation

We have art inorder not to die from the truth....

Art brings people together..It elicits different reactions and emotions but 
everyone can agree that it moves them to think ,to feel,and to act.I feel the pulse of
life when i move my arms and legs with the rythm of life...yes! Iam a dancer by heart!!

A child to a Dancer ; My journey

Every child is born with a talent.But those who identify the master in them brings out art to the world and hence happiness!Being born to a family that ...respects art and its beauty,I started my little moves at the age of 3! Life has taken me to different places but the passion to dance got along!Professionally trained by some of the best and graceful teachers,Srimathi Kalakshethra Sreelatha Namboothiri,Sri kalamandalam Parameswaran Nair and Srimathi kalamandalam Savithri,I could pave a way to the depth of my soul!And guess what! Dancing still keeps me going..

Milestones in the journey of dance!

Getting trained,as any other kid, started giving me the confidence to face my fears.I felt blessed each time I was on the stage.My schooling has a major part in this story!Being a Bhavanite, ...I never ran short of a chance to showcase my passion..I had a really memorable time being a trained bharatanatyam and mohiniyattam dancer in the school and feel gratified for being honoured the best of awards for it!I had the wonderful chance to perform and secure A grade in the CBSE state kalolsav, after being the district level winner. I was indeed lucky to have an opportunity to dance infront of very talented dance masters in Guruvayoor temple, a land filled with the eternal presence of Lord Krishna!

Being an actor... !!

Dance is not just about the moves..Classical dance portraits stories and has much more to do with expressions.. And when the right time came,I used my skill to become an actor!I had my first ...try in monoact, when I was 12years old and surprisingly achieved first prize in the school level competition.. From then,I always devoted my life to being an artist heart fully! I went for competitions, both district and state level and achieved several awards for my best monoact performances...I always tried to portray those sections of the society that was never noticed. I used my expressions to tell their story that was left unheard! And hence, I always feel thankful to be born as a dancer that brought the actor in me!
